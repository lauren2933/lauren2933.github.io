Why I Think Dementia Awarenss is Important

Over the summer my great grandmother passed away. She was a very important person in my life, and she helped raise me. She helped make me the person I am today. I now know she had a severe case of dementia. When she started forgetting simple things, like where she put her keys, I thought she was just forgetting because she was getting older. I didn't realize that was just the beginning of a progressive mental illness. She gradually forgt more and more until she could not take care of herself. I knew she had bad memory problems, but I did not want to accept it was dementia. I couldn't believe something I had vaguely heard of could effect my life so much. I think it is important to get more people, especcialy younger people, to learn about dementia, so it will not be as much of a surprise if their loved one has dementia. I wanted to learn more about dementia to try and seek closure, and I think that will be important for many other people as well.
